Quick Reference

Latitude (Horizontal Lines)

Measures North ↔ South position

Range: -90° to +90°

0° = Equator

Longitude (Vertical Lines)

Measures East ↔ West position

Range: -180° to +180°

0° = Prime Meridian

Key Learning Points:

  • Latitude runs horizontally (east-west lines) but measures north-south position
  • Longitude runs vertically (north-south lines) but measures east-west position
  • The Equator (0° latitude) divides Earth into Northern and Southern hemispheres
  • The Prime Meridian (0° longitude) divides Earth into Eastern and Western hemispheres
  • Coordinates are always written as (Latitude, Longitude)
